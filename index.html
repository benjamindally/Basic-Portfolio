Beginning Web Designer

When I was in college, I had to take a number of classes. Some I liked, others I didn't. But I'll never forget one professor. After class we were chatting about an upcoming assignment. Towards the end of the conversation, she said, "Benjamin, you seem to see a different world."

I don't know if she said that to all students who went to her with random questions, but it stuck with me. It also helped me realize that we all have a unique perspective, and we all see our own different world.

While I am unashamedly new to the world of web development, this site hopefully shows my potential, dedication, and hard work. It is an introduction to, not only me as a web developer, but me as a working professional.

Thank you for finding my page. I hope that it is a good introduction to who I am and why I am in this exciting field. I know that your time is very important, and you are spending it well by checking this site.
